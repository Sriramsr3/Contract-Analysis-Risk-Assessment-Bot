EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

Agreement between RapidTech Pvt Ltd and Employee.

1. Role and Duties
Employee shall perform duties assigned by employer.

2. Salary
Salary paid monthly.

3. Termination
Employer may terminate employee without notice or compensation.

4. Non-Compete
Employee cannot work with competitors for 2 years after exit.

5. IP Ownership
All employee-created work belongs exclusively to employer.

6. Jurisdiction
Bengaluru courts govern disputes.
