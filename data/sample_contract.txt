SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement is made between Alpha Retail Pvt Ltd and BrightTech Solutions.

1. Services
BrightTech Solutions agrees to provide software maintenance services for 12 months.

2. Payment Terms
Alpha Retail shall pay INR 5,00,000 annually. Payments delayed beyond 30 days incur penalties.

3. Termination
Alpha Retail may terminate the contract at any time without prior notice.

4. Indemnity
Vendor agrees to indemnify Alpha Retail against all losses or damages arising from service failure.

5. Auto Renewal
This agreement automatically renews annually unless terminated 60 days prior.

6. Jurisdiction
All disputes shall fall under Mumbai jurisdiction.
